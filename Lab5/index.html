About

Learn about how we got started.

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    It all started in a little village in Pakistan where a young boy named Mustafa had the idea to open a bike shop. He needed money to buy a plane ticket to Canada. Little did he know, he would found a trillion dollar company.

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    Mustafa, upon moving to Canada, officially opened up his business under the name "The Bicycle Shop". It was a huge success and beat out branks like Nike and Adidas within hours. Within 3 months, the company was billion dollar company.

  • December 2012

    Transition to Full Service

    The Bicycle Shop, however, only had 3 employees: Tinku, Tabby and Goob. The number of stores were limited due to employee constraints. Mustafa went on a huge campaign and hired 5000 employees so he could open stores in the 50 provinces in the USA.

  • July 2014

    Phase Two Expansion

    Deals were made with various large name brands such as Kanye East and Mohammad Ronaldo to garner worldwide attention. The company is current looking for international support to expand its customer base and rid the world of malfunctioning bikes.
